A smart solution for garbage management

Smart bin is an intelligent bin, able to detect its filling status, frequency and time of use through a constant scanning carried out by sensors, sending the collected data to a back-end software, via wireless network.

This system allows a selective and optimized collection program: the containers are collected only when strictly necessary.

Goals:

  • Sustainability: reduce air pollution caused by staff vehicles and encourage users in doing separate collection thanks to gamification system (points reward).
  • Ethics: in accordance with ethical code of waste disposal companies.
  • Optimization: it improves costs, time and human resources used in garbage management.
  • Users' satisfaction: qualitative improvement of the service ensuring immediate intervention and avoiding the accumulation of waste over the container.

Bins usage

This section has an organizing function in managing the work of the staff: it allows operators to monitor the status of the containers in real time and to intervene in a targeted way, only on those whose capacity has reached the maximum limit.

Each bin sends measurements related to its filling state (empty, a little full, almost full or completed full) and when a particular bin is full, a notification will automatically appear on the screen so that the operator will intervene precisely.
